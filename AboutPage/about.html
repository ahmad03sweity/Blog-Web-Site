Ahmad Sweity

Front-End-Develober

Ahmad Sweity is a computer engineering student at Palestine Polytechnic University with a deep passion for technology, especially in fields like ethical hacking, artificial intelligence, and the Internet of Things (IoT). He has a strong foundation in front-end development and is expanding his programming skills in JavaScript and Python. Currently, Ahmad is actively working on projects that integrate IoT devices with Microsoft Azure, including an innovative soil moisture monitoring system. This project involves Azure IoT Hub, Stream Analytics, and SQL services. He is also exploring embedded systems with ESP32 and is honing his skills in robotics by designing and simulating obstacle-avoiding robot cars on Tinkercad. Despite financial challenges, Ahmad remains dedicated to his studies and is keen to deepen his expertise in embedded systems, IoT, and AI. His drive for continuous learning and innovation is setting a promising path for a future in tech.
